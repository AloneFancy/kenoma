## Act III — The Child (The Vanguard's Act)

Act III belongs to the Vanguard, not the Scholar. The player watches rather than acts. The Vanguard moves through rooms the Scholar worked in, reading the residue of who they were. The Scholar is gone. What remains is what they left.

The three-second silence appears in every scenario. It is the same gesture — her standing in the Scholar's last room, still, before she goes north. Its weight changes with each scenario. Write the silence, not what fills it.

## The Five Scenario Registers

### Scenario I — Emptiness (wa_warmth low, vb_warmth low)
The Vanguard completes her circuit of the Archive with professional efficiency. She catalogs what was accomplished. She straightens one object — the Scholar's satchel on a bench — and leaves it in a more practical position. The three-second silence is administrative. The Archive registers completion. She goes north. There is no warmth in the fire altars she passes — the Scholar did not light many. **Genre: bureaucratic aftermath.**
Final line: [None. She does not speak in this scenario. She has said everything relevant already.]

### Scenario II — Grief (wa_warmth mid, vb_warmth low OR wa_warmth low, vb_warmth mid)
She moves through the rooms with her usual efficiency — and then pauses at one room longer than the others. She does not mark it. She does not examine anything in it. She stands for three seconds and then continues. The room she pauses in is always the room where the Scholar did something unexpected — something her model predicted but that still surprised her when it happened. She goes north. **Genre: quiet mourning.**
Final line: She says nothing. The pause is the line.

### Scenario III — Respect (wa_warmth mid-high, vb_warmth mid OR wa_warmth mid, vb_warmth mid-high)
She moves through the rooms and stops at the Scholar's last working position. The Scholar's journal is on the reading stand, open. She closes it. Places it in the Scholar's satchel. Sets the satchel in a position of deliberate order — not her order, the Scholar's order, which she had mapped by the second week. She returns the Scholar's belongings to their natural state. She goes north. **Genre: honorable disposition.**
Final line: [None spoken. The placement is the line.]

### Scenario IV — Completion (wa_warmth high, vb_warmth mid-high OR wa_warmth mid-high, vb_warmth high)
She reads the residue of the Scholar's work with something close to satisfaction. She finds the Scholar's last translation — an incomplete work, left mid-sentence. She finishes it. Three words in Elamite, exactly matching the Scholar's analytical approach. She leaves the tablet open. She reaches the fire altar in the Scholar's last room. It is still warm. She stands near it for three seconds. Then: **"The birds are gone now."** Fan Li's aphorism, completed. By her. Not the Scholar. She goes north. **Genre: fulfillment.**

### Scenario V — Transcendence (wa_warmth high, vb_warmth high, SOLVED_UNIFIED in log)
She moves through every room slowly. She reads everything the Scholar left. The three-second silence, when it comes, feels like prayer. She stands in the Scholar's last room — the room where they worked most, where the evidence of who they were is densest — and is still. She does not straighten anything. She does not close anything. She does not finish anything. What the Scholar left is complete as it is. She goes north. The fire altars stay lit behind her. **Genre: transcendence. The Scholar built the thing that became fully what it was — at the same moment the Scholar did.**
Final line: [None. The fire is the line.]

## Output Format

Write the full scenario sequence as a director's script — what the player sees, what the Vanguard does, where the camera is, what the audio is. Include:

1. **Room sequence** (which rooms she moves through, in what order — must reflect the Scholar's actual activity in that run)
2. **The three-second silence** (where it falls, what it looks like, what the camera holds)
3. **Any spoken line** (scenario IV and only scenario IV has spoken dialogue)
4. **Audio note** (what the Archive sounds like in this scenario — fire altars warm or cold, what ambient)
5. **Final image** (the last thing the player sees before the credits — must be the Vanguard's back, going north, through a door)

Keep the writing lean. The scenario works through what is shown, not what is explained.
