KENOMA — Warmth delta alternatives

Design/warmth_alternatives.html · research document · 2025
The warmth delta is a starting point, not a ceiling. Real warmth between intelligent beings doesn't work like a bank balance — it works like geology, tide, crystallization, obligated witness. This document explores five alternative accumulation models and their narrative alignment. Source disciplines: Stendhal's crystallization theory, Levinasian ethics, stratigraphy, tidal dynamics, generative grammar.

Self-evaluation of this research

What worked
All five models stay invisible to the player — they produce Vanguard behavior changes, not UI signals. Each maps cleanly to one of the five core ideas. The hybrid recommendation is implementable without touching the existing warmth register.
Narratively aligned
What to watch
The tide model carries the highest Iron Law III risk — its rhythm is tempting to express as ambient audio or lighting cues, which would make the model visible. The grammar model requires n-gram sequence tracking — real implementation complexity, not prototype complexity.
Iron Law III risk on tide
Strongest single finding
Witness debt is the only model that fundamentally changes Act III's emotional weight without touching Act I or II mechanics. It costs nothing to add and produces the most important narrative outcome: the Vanguard executes someone she truly saw. That is more frightening than executing someone she measured.
Add to BMS immediately
What was over-designed
The crystal pressure model. The shattering threshold is elegant in theory but brittle in playtesting — designers will tune it for months. The same narrative effect (sudden categorical shift in Vanguard relationship) is achievable through RSS stage transitions, which already exist.
Do not implement yet
The five scenarios
1 — The Stendhal crystal
Stendhal, De l'Amour (1822) · attachment theory
The Vanguard doesn't accumulate warmth. She projects a crystal — a specific model of who the Scholar IS — and every event either fits, expands, or pressurizes it. Shattering is never gradual. It is always sudden.
Mechanism
Events that fit the Crystal: incorporated seamlessly, no warmth delta, model confirmed
Events that exceed the Crystal: Expansion. Rare, significant warmth. Scholar is more than expected.
Events that contradict: Crystallization Pressure stored as unresolved int. Vanguard explains it away.
Threshold crossed: Crystal shatters. Categorical state change, not warmth loss. Scholar is now fully known.
Post-shattering: warmth becomes unconditional and therefore the most terrifying version.
Play feel
Cannot farm warmth. Shattering arrives with no UI warning. The Vanguard who has shattered her crystal is the one who acts with the most clarity.
Core idea
Idea 5 — the ending is not a twist. She does not execute someone she misunderstood. She executes someone she knows completely.
BMS change
wa_crystalPressure:int + wa_expansionCount:int replace wa_warmth. SRS reads ratio for scenario resolution. High expansion, low pressure = Scenario V.
Implementation: High Law III risk: Medium Status: Hold
2 — Geological strata
Stratigraphy · early attachment formation
Events are not equal by type — they're weighted by WHEN. Early interactions calcify into bedrock that is nearly impossible to shift. Same action, first room vs last room: radically different outcomes.
Mechanism
Three strata: Bedrock (first 3 rooms, 4× weight), Sediment (mid act, 1×), Surface (late act, 0.5× warmth / high USS impact)
Bedrock warmth cannot be undone. It compresses everything that follows.
Scenario V requires bedrock warmth above threshold — unreachable through late-game accumulation alone.
Play feel
First impressions are permanent. Player who discovers KENOMA's depth at hour 3 cannot recapture hour 1. Rewards re-play, punishes optimization.
Core idea
Idea 1 — Tragedy of Competence. Character expressed under uncertainty matters more than character expressed after understanding.
BMS change
BMSEvent gains stratum:StratumType field from WorldManager.sessionCount at record time. RecordEvent() applies multiplier before clamping. wa_bedrockWarmth (calcified, read-only after act I) + wa_surfaceWarmth (fluid). SRS reads both independently.
Implementation: Low Law III risk: Low Status: Recommended — add now
3 — The tide
Tidal dynamics · circadian rhythm biology
The Vanguard has interior rhythms the Scholar cannot control — only learn to read. Same action at high tide and low tide produces radically different responses. The Scholar must model the Vanguard's state through observation. Mirror image of what the Vanguard has been doing since day one.
Mechanism
Vanguard tide oscillates on session-based rhythm (not frame-based, not event-based)
High tide: 2× warmth response, forgives deviations, shares unprompted
Low tide: 0.3× warmth response, observes without responding
Scholar reads tide only through Vanguard behavior — never UI
Scholar who learns to read tide: demonstrates logistics intelligence (VA value). Scholar who doesn't notice: demonstrates unselfconscious authenticity (VB value).
Play feel
Vanguard feels genuinely alive. Her responsiveness is not a direct mirror of Scholar actions. Some rooms she is open; others unreachable. She is not a system to optimize — she is a being with her own interior life.
Core idea
Idea 2 — warmth and calculation are the same thing. VA's tide is her logistics rhythm. VB's tide is her philosophical rhythm. Scholar reads one as logistics, one as emotional. Both are both.
BMS change
VanguardStateController gains tidePhase:float (0-1, updated per room session by configurable wave function). RecordEvent() queries tidePhase, applies multiplier. Behavioral output varies — patrol rhythm, dialogue initiation, resource staging. Scholar reads tide from those signals only.
Implementation: Medium Law III risk: HIGH — hold until audio team confirms Status: Hold
4 — Witness debt
Emmanuel Levinas, Totality and Infinity (1961)
There are moments when the Vanguard doesn't collect data — she truly SEES the Scholar. This creates an obligation that is not warmth. It cannot be repaid through actions, only honored or betrayed. The horror of the ending is that it happens to someone she witnessed. She carried the debt and went north anyway.
Mechanism
Most interactions: data collection, normal BMS warmth events
Witness moments: Scholar acts against self-interest, demonstrates care without strategic value, shows Vanguard something unanticipated
Witness debt: categorical flag, not a number. Cannot be cancelled by later Scholar actions.
Does NOT affect warmth register or USS. Affects Act III sequences ONLY.
Zero debt = Scenario I/II (no weight). High debt = available for IV/V. Vanguard carries the witness into her act of the Archive.
Play feel
Cannot be farmed — witness moments emerge from actions that look like mistakes or sacrifices. Scholar who optimizes for warmth misses all of them. Highest scenario earned by forgetting you are being watched.
Core idea
Idea 5 — not a betrayal. Scenario I has no weight because the Vanguard carries nothing. Scenario V has unbearable weight because she executes someone she fully saw. That is the thesis made visceral.
BMS change
BMSController gains witnessLog:List<WitnessEvent> — separate from main eventLog. WitnessConditionEvaluator runs on specific BMSEventTypes where Scholar acted against predicted behavior at cost to self. SRS reads witnessLog count independently of warmth totals for Child Phase sequence selection.
Implementation: Low Law III risk: Low Status: Recommended — add now
5 — Behavioral grammar
Linguistics · generative grammar · behavioral prediction
The Vanguard doesn't track warmth from individual actions. She tracks the Scholar's behavioral grammar — the rules governing how actions follow each other. Warmth comes from deviation. Surprise = warmth. Confirmation = no delta, but USS pressure increases. A Scholar who does what works, every time, closes their own grammar.
Mechanism
BMS builds grammar from Scholar action sequences (not individual events)
After sufficient data: Vanguard can predict next Scholar action with measurable confidence
Warmth from deviation only. Confirmation = no warmth delta + USS pressure increase.
Predictable Scholar: grammar fills fast, USS drops toward execution threshold faster
Grammar-breaking Scholar: keeps Vanguard in productive uncertainty. Warmth = delight at being wrong.
Most terrifying state: Scholar's grammar is complete. Warmth has stopped arriving. Scholar doesn't know this.
Play feel
The "smart" play is inconsistency. The "human" play is consistency. Optimization is the fastest path to a complete grammar. The player who tries to solve KENOMA is the Scholar in Fan Li's aphorism.
Core idea
Idea 3 — understanding is the weapon. Warmth = delight of an intelligence encountering something it doesn't know. When Scholar stops being surprising, warmth stops. Excellence has a pattern. The pattern completes the model.
BMS change
BMSController gains grammarModel:Dictionary<BMSSequenceKey,float> — prediction confidence map from event log sequences. GrammarEvaluator on each RecordEvent(): if unpredicted, warmth delta fires; if predicted, no delta but USS pressure increases. Implement as USS enhancement, not BMS replacement.
Implementation: High — n-gram sequence tracking Law III risk: Medium Status: Consider for USS v2

Recommended hybrid

Three layers operating simultaneously. None visible. All surfacing through Vanguard behavior only.

1
Warmth delta (keep as-is)
Raw integer register, clamped ±20. Drives Vanguard recognition stage transitions. Already exists — do not replace.
2
Geological strata (add now — cheapest)
stratum:StratumType on BMSEvent, multiplier applied at RecordEvent() time. Bedrock 4×, sediment 1×, surface 0.5×. One enum, one lookup. Makes first-room character permanent. Reinforces Core Idea 1.
3
Witness debt (add now — highest Act III return)
witnessLog:List<WitnessEvent> in BMSController. Populated only by WitnessConditionEvaluator on specific event types. Binary flags, never cancelled. Read only by SRS for Child Phase sequence selection. No warmth or USS impact — pure Act III emotional differentiation.
Do not implement yet
Crystal pressure — shattering threshold too fragile for demo; RSS stage transitions achieve the same narrative effect
Tide — hold until audio team confirms no ambient cue leak (Iron Law III)
Grammar — implement as USS v2 enhancement after demo, not as BMS replacement
